🔥 My First-ever **#KubeCon + #CloudNativeCon India 2025** - what an incredible experience! 💡

🚀 Learned so much about **#Observability**, deploying & scaling LLMs in Kubernetes (Official), and got to meet some amazing folks:

- 🎙️ Atulpriya - Technical Storyteller & CNCF Ambassador
- 📈 Steve Flanders - OpenTelemetry Contributor
- 📊 Saiyam Pathak - Observability Keynote Speaker
- 🚀 Yongkang- Kubestronaut & Founder @ KSUG.AI – KubeSmart & AI User Group
- …and so many inspiring industry experts & open-source contributors!

🙌 Huge thanks to **Cloud Native Computing Foundation (CNCF)** for bringing this vibrant community together.

And to all the 𝗰𝘂𝗿𝗶𝗼𝘂𝘀 minds who visited our EDB booth to explore **#CloudNativePG** and **#EDBPostgresAI**, your energy & excitement took the vibe to another level! 🚀✨
